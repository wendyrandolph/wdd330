Transforms

Lets you translate, rotate, scale and/or skew any element on a page.

Translation - move up, down, left or right.

translate:(x,y) plug in 45px, -45px means that you take x from the left that many pixels
from the left, and that you take y that many pixels from the top.
If you only want to move vertically or horizontally then it looks like
transform: translateX(45px) or transform: translateY(-30px)

Scaling

scale(x,y) scales horizontally then vertically. Example: transorm: scale(1.5, .25)
If you only want to scale on one axis or the other you will use:

  • scaleX(x) or scaleY(y)

    • Rotation -

    rotate() rotates around a point of origin by a specified angle value.
    Point of origin is the element's center. Angles are declared by degrees.

    Skew

    skew(x,y) ex. transform: skew(15deg, 4deg).
    Choose your order carefully.

    Transistions:

    Allows the value of CSS to change over time. Transition has 4 parts:

  • transition_property = defines CSS properties that should transition.
  • transition_duration = sets how long a transition will take.
  • transition_timing_function = controls the pace of the transition
  • transition_delay = introduce a delay before the transition begins.
